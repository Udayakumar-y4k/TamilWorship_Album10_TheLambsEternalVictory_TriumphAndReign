# My God, My God, Why Have You Forsaken Me? - என் தேவனே என் தேவனே ஏன் என்னைக் கைவிட்டீர்?

This anguished cry echoes Psalm 22 — David’s lament, prophetically fulfilled by Jesus on the cross (Matt 27:46). From forsakenness to victory — enemies defeated, nations worship, eternal reign. In the little season, we share Christ’s suffering, but His resurrection guarantees our triumph (Rom 8:17; Rev 21:7).
Closing Prayer for the Listener

In our anguish, hear our cry.
Turn lament to praise, suffering to victory.
