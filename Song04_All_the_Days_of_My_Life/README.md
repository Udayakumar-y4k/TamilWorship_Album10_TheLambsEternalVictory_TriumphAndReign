# All my living days, Your goodness follows me - என் ஜீவனுள்ள நாட்களெல்லாம்

This song of perfect provision and eternal security echoes Psalm 23 — the Lord as Shepherd, preparing table, anointing head, goodness and mercy following, dwelling in His house forever. In the eternal day, this is fulfilled — the Lamb shepherds us by living waters, no more death or fear (Revelation 7:17; 21:4).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, Good Shepherd and eternal King,
thank You for Your goodness and mercy that follow us all our days.
Anoint our heads, overflow our cups, lead us beside still waters.
In the eternal day, let us dwell in Your house forever.
Come quickly, our Shepherd.
