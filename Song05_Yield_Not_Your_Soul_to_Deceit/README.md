# Yield Not Your Soul to Deceit - ஆத்துமாவை மாயைக்கு ஒப்புக் கொடாதே

Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, King of glory with many crowns,
thank You for clean hands and pure hearts through Your blood.
Lift us to Your holy hill.
In the eternal day, let us stand in Your presence forever.
Come quickly, King of glory.
